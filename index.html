Traffic Counter

Count vehicles at an intersection by type, turning movement, and approach. Select your intersection layout, choose vehicle types, and set a time interval. Tap each vehicle button as it passes. Long-press to correct a miscount. Data auto-saves at each interval.

Count boarding and alighting passengers at a PT stop. Add the bus/tram lines that pass your stop. When a vehicle arrives, tap its line, then tap Boarding/Alighting for each passenger (or switch to number entry for busy stops). Tap Done to log the vehicle. Data auto-saves at each interval.

Select 2 or more .xlsx files exported by students counting at the same intersection. Files will be merged by combining approaches and summing overlapping data.
